Live Demo - Lsos Donation Reminder

Open the developer console of your browser to see the donation-reminder.

Note that:

  • The donation-reminder is only shown on Chromium-based browsers; you won't be able to see it on Firefox.
  • The donation-reminder is normally not shown in production. This demo is an exception.
  • The donation-reminder is shown in development; the lsos remove command is about removing the donation-reminder while developing.
